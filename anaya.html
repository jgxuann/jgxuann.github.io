"當我們談論永恆時"


With the development of society and technology, we can use the camera, and video recorder to capture unforgettable moments. However, how can we breathe the air at that moment? If we will forget the story about the pictures or photos? Nothing is eternal expects time, and what we only can do is make the time 'SLOWER'.

Works

Anselm Reyle said "I was fascinated by the reflection of light and shadow when I bought a small wind chimes toys at the Christmas market in Berlin 10 years ago, and I thought of the dynamic art of the 1960s."

The shape of 'Wind Chimes' is taken from the geometric metal decoration the artist procured from the Berlin Christmas market ten years ago. After being enlarged dozens of times, the wind chimes have transformed from handicrafts to artwork. Looking down from the circular window, the big wind chimes rotate slightly with the wind, as if bringing the author back to that Christmas day ten years ago. I happened to be there during the Christmas season. In the bleak breeze, I realized the meaning of the 'DAILY-LIFE'S ART'.

As its name shown to us 'After Forever' , we want to find the meaning of eternity. The visitors with kinds of backgrounds come from the different areas, and each one will have the different answer of this question. Just like this work, and you can see the calm lake in front of you eyes if you sit here and watch its turing on the celling. As if the time is slow down, when we are watching it. It shadow changes every second with its turning, and we will feel the flow of time concretely in another way.

Useful & Unuseful

'ART' appears in our life with high frequency, and its bar is getting lower and lower, which means more and more people in China can realize the art, and enjoy it. The discussion about the value of art is very hot, and some think it should attach some additional human significance, but others just enjoy it. From my perspective, everything in this world has its meaning, and everyone has different viewpoints on the same thing.

Just like the word of Real, " Why art can't be decoration? " We don't need to give anything a lebal, just look, enjoy, and think the unique meaning of the art belongs to yourself.

As an undergraduate, computer science, I always think the question what is Art? I know it looks a little stupid, but I keep falling into the whirlpool. I told myself, 'Stop thinking that!!!'. This is a road without the END and as that word 'There are a thousand Hamlets in a thousand people's eyes.' Not everything has the answer, and just explore every minutes on your own road. Almost nothing is eternal, but keep exploring, feeling, and thinking are the eternal things in one's life.
