Hi, my name is Guanxuan (Grayson) Jiang, and I am an MPhil Student in the Computational Media and Art Thrust at the Information Hub, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). I am supervised by Prof. Pan Hui and Prof. Fugee Tsung.

My research lies at the intersection of Behavior Modelling and Emgering Technology – with a special focus on how human interact with Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Large Langugae Model and so forth. My research interests include Human-Machine Interaction, Behavior Modelling, VR/AR, Computational Intelligence Interaction and Human Centred Computing.

Previously, I completed my BSc. (Hons) in Computer Science at University of Sheffield and Chinese University of Hong Kong. I also spent time at Peking Univerisity and Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University.
