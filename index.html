Guanxuan (Grayson) Jiang

Feel free to reach out to me via gjiang240@connect.hkust-gz.edu.cn



My name is Guanxuan Jiang (蒋冠宣), and I am an MPhil (Master of Philosophy) student in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) with a full scholarship. My research interests are in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Human Behavior, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR). I am member of Center for Metaverse and Computational Creativity (MC2) Lab. I obtained my Bachelor of Science with Honour from the University of Sheffield in the department of Computer Science with one-year Study Abroad at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supported by the Global Opportunity Organization and the Turning Scheme.
