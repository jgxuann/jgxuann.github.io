"Deja Vu"-似曾相識

Have you noticed that some scenes that happened in your memory? To be honest, this picture made me remember my childhood, and I think I have dreamed this ever. Actually, this phenomenon is called Effect of the hippocampus. It's people's perception of what's happening in other multiverse on the same timeline. Misperceiving or judging immediate information as "a picture in memory " due to a brain processing error.
